Sound Poetry at the Electronic Poetry Center

The Electronic Poetry Center, published out of the State University of New York at Buffalo, is one of the largest resources for twentieth-century and contemporary poetry on the internet.

Sound Poetry at UbuWeb

UbuWeb, currently off-line for the summer, is moving its extensive media files to the servers at WFMU radio (FM 91.1). Transfer should be complete by fall.

Audio Archives at the Lannan Foundation

Lannan Foundation has an extensive audio library drawn from literary readings and discussions hosted by the center, from recorded installments of the literary interview radio program "Bookworm," as well as from other areas. As stated on the site, "Lannan's literary program was established in 1987 to support the creation of exceptional poetry and prose written originally in the English language and to increase the audience for contemporary literature."

"Lannan Foundation is a family foundation dedicated to cultural freedom, diversity and creativity through projects which support exceptional contemporary artists and writers, as well as inspired Native activists in rural indigenous communities."
