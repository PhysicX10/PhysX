Goal The PhysX project was initiated at the 2015 Dalton Hackathon for the purpose of providing a means through which students that are taking physics can have a resource that allows them to visualize the problems that they are assigned and offers solutions and explanations for those problems. Our program implements the Wolfram API to compute solutions for problems that involve projectile motion, collision, and rotational movement. In the future, we hope to expand PhysX to incorporate other subjects of physics and provide practical help in those areas as well.
