Reading Rants: Out of the Ordinary Teen Booklists!

(Copyright: Jennifer Hubert -- 1998 - 2004 All Rights Reserved)

An idiosyncratic yet terrific resource aimed specifically at readers from 6th to 12th grade. Jennifer Hubert, Middle School Librarian at the Little Red School House & Elisabeth Irwin High School in Greenwich Village in Manhattan, has categorized her favorite books into sections such as"Hip History," "Reality Bytes: Non-fiction about teens, for teens," "Coolest Classics," "Word UP: Poetry both by and for teenagers," and many others.
